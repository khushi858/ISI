Crate Types & Construction Standards

Standard Wooden Crates

Construction: Heavy-duty pinewood and rubberwood construction with reinforced corner posts and cross-bracing. Available in open-top design for easy loading, closed design with removable panels for complete protection, and skeleton crates with minimal framing for lightweight shipments. Thickness ranges from 18mm to 25mm depending on load requirements.

Load Capacity: Designed to support static loads from 500kg to 5000kg (5 tons) with safety factor of 4:1. Floor boards spaced at optimal intervals for forklift compatibility. Reinforced base construction with double-layer flooring for heavy machinery transport. Corner protectors and edge guards prevent damage during handling.

Plywood Boxes & Cases

Material Specifications: Marine-grade plywood (IS:710 BWP) with thickness 12mm, 15mm, or 18mm offering superior moisture resistance and dimensional stability. Constructed with tongue-and-groove joints sealed with waterproof adhesive. Internal blocking provides additional strength for heavy items. Smooth interior finish prevents product damage.

Design Features: Modular construction allows custom sizing from 300x300x300mm to 3000x2000x2000mm. Integrated lifting points with steel reinforcement for crane handling. Weatherproof sealing with rubber gaskets for ocean freight. Optional internal cushioning with foam inserts or corrugated dividers for fragile cargo protection.

ISPM-15 Certified Export Crates

Compliance Standards: International Standards for Phytosanitary Measures No. 15 (ISPM-15) certified for all wood packaging materials. Heat treatment (HT) process at 56¬∞C core temperature for minimum 30 minutes eliminates pests and diseases. Methyl bromide fumigation (MB) available for specific country requirements. Permanent IPPC stamps and certification documents included.

Export Features: Kiln-dried lumber with moisture content below 20% prevents warping during transit. Corner brackets and steel strapping integration points ensure secure closure. Stencil-ready surface for shipping marks, handling instructions, and destination labels. Collapsible designs available for return shipping cost reduction up to 80%.

Custom-Built Crates

Engineering Services: CAD-designed crates tailored to exact product dimensions and weight distribution. Load analysis and stress testing ensure optimal wood selection and reinforcement placement. 3D modeling visualization provided before manufacturing. Field-proven designs for machinery, electronics, artwork, and automotive components.

Specialized Construction: Shock-absorbing bases with spring-loaded mounts for vibration-sensitive equipment. Climate-controlled packaging with desiccant integration and vapor barrier lining for humidity control. Anti-static interior coating for electronic components. Tilt indicators and shock sensors can be integrated for monitoring during transport.

Technical Specifications & Materials

Wood Material Options

Pinewood: Lightweight yet strong, ideal for general export packaging. Density 500-550 kg/m¬≥ provides excellent nail-holding capacity. Natural resin content offers inherent moisture resistance. Cost-effective solution for standard shipping containers with load capacity up to 2 tons per crate.

Rubberwood: Sustainable hardwood with superior strength-to-weight ratio. Density 600-650 kg/m¬≥ suitable for heavy industrial machinery packaging. Tight grain structure resists splitting during nailing and handling. Excellent dimensional stability across temperature variations from -20¬∞C to +50¬∞C.

Marine Plywood: Waterproof boil-resistant (BWP) grade with phenolic resin bonding. 7-ply or 9-ply construction maintains integrity even when exposed to rain or humidity. Zero delamination guarantee for 12-month storage period. Ideal for sea freight requiring long-duration moisture protection.

Hardwood Options: Teak, sal, and acacia for ultra-heavy-duty applications exceeding 5-ton loads. High-density hardwoods (700-900 kg/m¬≥) resist compression and impact damage. Premium choice for valuable machinery and long-term reusable packaging solutions. Custom milling available for precise tolerances.

Fastening & Hardware

Nails & Screws: Galvanized ring-shank nails (2.5"-4") provide 300% higher pull-out resistance than smooth nails. Corrosion-resistant coating ensures 5+ year service life in outdoor storage. Deck screws (#8 to #12) with coarse threads for plywood assembly. Pneumatic nailing for consistent, rapid production.

Steel Reinforcement: Corner brackets (2mm-5mm thickness) with zinc plating prevent joint separation under shock loads. Steel strapping bands (16mm-32mm width) with tensile strength 1000-2000N provide external compression. Integrated lifting eyes (WLL 500kg-5000kg) with load certification enable safe crane handling.

Locking Mechanisms: Hasp and staple closures with padlock compatibility for security during transit. Butterfly latches for quick-access service panels. Cam locks for repeated opening/closing in reusable crates. Tamper-evident seals available with serial number tracking for high-value cargo.

Treatment & Certification

Heat Treatment (HT): ISPM-15 compliant kiln treatment at 56¬∞C core temperature for 30+ minutes verified with calibrated probes. Process eliminates bark beetles, pine wilt nematodes, and other quarantine pests. IPPC-certified stamps applied with permanent ink indicating treatment date, facility code, and country of origin.

Fumigation (MB): Methyl bromide gas treatment at 48g/m¬≥ concentration for 24-hour exposure period. Required for specific countries including Australia, New Zealand, and China. Chamber fumigation with gas concentration monitoring ensures complete penetration. Phytosanitary certificates issued by authorized government agencies.

Quality Documentation: Material certificates tracing wood source and treatment compliance. Load test reports with photographs demonstrating crate integrity under simulated transport conditions. Packaging declaration forms for customs clearance. ISO 9001:2015 certified manufacturing process with batch traceability.

Environmental & Sustainability

Sustainable Sourcing: FSC-certified (Forest Stewardship Council) wood from responsibly managed forests. Chain-of-custody documentation verifying legal harvesting practices. Rubberwood sourced from plantation trees at end of latex-producing life, promoting circular economy. Zero old-growth or endangered species wood used.

Recyclability: 100% biodegradable and recyclable natural wood material. Crates can be disassembled and wood reused for pallets, furniture, or biomass fuel. Metal hardware removed and recycled separately. Repairability extends service life up to 10+ uses for reusable packaging programs reducing waste by 90%.

Steel Pallet Types & Configurations

Standard Steel Pallets

Construction: Welded steel construction with 50mm x 50mm or 60mm x 60mm RHS (Rectangular Hollow Section) frame providing superior rigidity and load distribution. Top deck constructed from 3mm-5mm MS plate or perforated sheet with drainage holes. Four-way entry design allows forklift access from all sides. Corner protectors and edge reinforcement prevent damage during handling.

Load Specifications: Static load capacity 1000kg to 3000kg with safety factor 3:1 certified by load testing. Dynamic load capacity (in-transit) rated at 800kg to 2500kg. Racking load capacity when supported on warehouse beams: 750kg to 2000kg. Deck deflection under maximum load: less than 10mm ensuring product stability.

Stackable Steel Pallets

Design Features: Integrated corner posts (100mm x 100mm angle or box section) enable vertical stacking up to 6 levels high when loaded, 20+ levels when empty. Interlocking guides ensure stable stacking with anti-slip features. Total stack height optimization maximizes warehouse vertical space utilization by 300%. Post height: 150mm, 200mm, or custom dimensions.

Versatility: Removable post design converts stackable pallet to standard flat pallet for shipping flexibility. Quick-release pins or bolted connections allow tool-free post removal in under 2 minutes. Nesting capability when posts removed reduces return logistics costs by 75%. Universal foot design compatible with conveyor systems, AGVs, and automated storage systems.

Collapsible/Foldable Steel Pallets

Folding Mechanism: Hinged side panels and gates fold flat reducing height from 150mm to 50mm (67% space reduction). Spring-loaded locking pins secure panels in both deployed and collapsed positions. No tools required for transformation - single operator can collapse pallet in 30 seconds. Wear-resistant hinges rated for 10,000+ fold cycles.

Logistics Efficiency: Collapsed pallets stack 3x higher during return shipping reducing freight costs by 65%. Ideal for one-way export shipments where pallet return is necessary. Expanded pallet provides full rigidity and load capacity identical to non-folding units. Popular for automotive, retail distribution, and seasonal shipping applications.

Custom Steel Frames & Stillages

Engineering Design: CAD-modeled frames tailored to specific product dimensions and weight profiles. Tube-frame construction with mesh sides provides visibility and ventilation for packaged goods. Half-drop gates and full-drop gates enable easy front or side loading with forklifts. Base frames compatible with standard pallet dimensions (1200x1000mm, 1200x800mm) for logistics integration.

Specialized Applications: Automotive industry frames with contoured supports for engines, transmissions, and body panels. Coil frames with saddle supports for steel/aluminum coil transport. Tire racks with vertical storage slots. Glass frames with A-frame dividers and rubber padding. Textile racks with hanging bars for garment industry. Pharmaceutical industry GMP-compliant stainless steel frames.

Technical Specifications & Materials

Material Options

Mild Steel (MS): ASTM A36 or IS:2062 grade with yield strength 250 MPa, tensile strength 400-550 MPa. Cost-effective solution for general industrial applications with indoor storage. Requires protective coating (paint/powder) for corrosion resistance. Common for pallets with 3-7 year service life in controlled environments.

Galvanized Steel: Hot-dip galvanized (HDG) coating per ASTM A123 with minimum 85 microns zinc layer. Superior corrosion protection for outdoor storage, coastal environments, and marine shipping. 15-25 year service life without rust. Pre-galvanized steel (275 g/m¬≤ coating) available for lighter-duty applications at reduced cost.

Powder Coated Steel: Electrostatic powder coating in various colors (RAL standards) with 80-120 micron thickness. Excellent chip and scratch resistance with UV stability. Baked finish at 180-200¬∞C creates durable, attractive surface. Popular for retail and automotive industries requiring brand color matching. 5-10 year exterior durability.

Stainless Steel: SS304/SS316 grades for food processing, pharmaceutical, and chemical industries. Corrosion-proof and washable for hygiene-critical applications. Fully welded construction with ground seams eliminates bacterial harborage points. FDA-compliant materials certification available. 20+ year service life with minimal maintenance.

Dimensional Standards

International Sizes: Euro pallet 1200x800mm for European markets and standard shipping containers. North American 1219x1016mm (48"x40") for US logistics networks. Asian standard 1100x1100mm for square packaging compatibility. Australian 1165x1165mm for local pallet pooling systems. Custom dimensions from 800x600mm to 1500x1200mm available.

Height Variations: Low-profile 80mm-100mm height for maximum container utilization and low-clearance AGV compatibility. Standard 120mm-150mm height provides optimal forklift clearance and pallet jack access. High-profile 180mm-200mm for extra clearance on uneven surfaces or damaged warehouse floors. Custom heights accommodate specific automated handling equipment requirements.

Welding & Fabrication Quality

Welding Standards: MIG/MAG welding per AWS D1.1 structural welding code with full penetration welds at critical stress points. 100% visual inspection and random destructive testing verify weld integrity. Certified welders with documented training records. Weld spatter removal and grinding for smooth, snag-free surfaces protecting product and handler safety.

Surface Preparation: Shot blasting or acid pickling removes mill scale and contaminants before coating application. Degreasing ensures optimal paint adhesion. Sharp edges rounded to R3-R5 radius prevents packaging damage and worker injury. Burr-free fabrication with deburred holes and smooth cut edges. Final inspection per ISO 9001:2015 quality management standards.

Drop tests from 300mm-500mm height assess impact resistance. Fatigue testing with 1000+ load cycles confirms durability for repeated use applications.

Dimensional inspection reports with tolerance verification (¬±2mm). Coating thickness measurements with calibrated gauges. Shrink Film Types & Specifications

Stretch Wrap Film

Material Composition: Linear Low-Density Polyethylene (LLDPE) with multi-layer coextrusion providing superior puncture resistance and load-holding force. Available in blown film (high-clarity, strong) and cast film (quiet unwind, consistent). Stretch capacity 200%-300% (pre-stretch) reduces film consumption by 50% while maintaining load security.

Thickness & Performance: Standard gauge 12-23 microns (50-90 gauge) for general pallets, heavy-duty 25-35 microns for sharp-edged or unstable loads. Cling properties: single-sided cling adheres to itself preventing load shifting, double-sided cling for bundle wrapping. Load retention force 3-8 kg maintains pallet integrity during transport and storage.

Heat Shrink Film

Shrink Properties: Polyolefin (POF) or PVC film shrinks 40-60% when heated to 120-160¬∞C, conforming tightly to product contours. Cross-linked polyethylene for high-temperature applications. Biaxially oriented shrink film provides uniform shrinkage in all directions preventing warping. Crystal-clear transparency showcases packaged products for retail display.

Application Methods: Manual shrink wrap with heat gun for small batches, semi-automatic L-bar sealers for medium production, fully automatic shrink tunnel systems processing 20-50 pallets/hour. Center-folded or single-wound rolls available. Perforation patterns enable steam venting during shrink process preventing air pockets and wrinkles.

VCI (Vapor Corrosion Inhibitor) Film

Corrosion Protection: Polyethylene film impregnated with volatile corrosion inhibitors (VCI) releases protective vapor molecules forming invisible mono-molecular layer on metal surfaces. Prevents rust, tarnish, and oxidation for up to 24 months in sealed packaging. Protects ferrous and non-ferrous metals including steel, aluminum, copper, brass without surface preparation.

Technical Specifications: Available in pink/blue tinted film for easy identification of corrosion-protected packaging. Effective protection range 60cm from film surface in enclosed spaces. No oily residue - parts ready for immediate use after unpacking. FDA/USDA compliant formulations for food industry equipment. Salt spray test results: 500+ hours corrosion-free per ASTM B117.

Specialized Films & Coatings

UV Protection Film: UV-stabilized polyethylene blocks 95%+ harmful UV radiation extending outdoor storage life from 3 months to 12+ months. Prevents polymer degradation, color fading, and brittleness. Essential for construction materials, agricultural products, and outdoor equipment storage. Available in black, white, or clear with UV absorbers/stabilizers.

Anti-Static Film: Dissipative or conductive polyethylene prevents static electricity buildup protecting sensitive electronics. Surface resistivity 10^8 to 10^11 ohms/square meets ESD safe packaging standards. Pink or black colored for identification. Ideal for PCB boards, semiconductors, LCD panels, and electronic components in clean room environments.

Breathable Film: Micro-perforated film allows moisture vapor transmission preventing condensation buildup while blocking liquid water penetration. Prevents mold and mildew on hygroscopic products like paper, textiles, and food grains. Perforation density customizable from 0.5% to 5% open area. Temperature-fluctuation protection for shipping containers and outdoor storage.

Technical Specifications & Performance

Film Properties & Testing

Mechanical Strength: Tensile strength 20-60 N/mm¬≤ (ASTM D882) ensures film doesn't tear during wrapping. Elongation at break 300-700% provides stretch without failure. Dart drop impact test 150-500g measures puncture resistance against sharp objects. Tear propagation resistance 50-200 N/mm prevents small punctures from enlarging during handling.

Optical Properties: Haze measurement 2-15% (ASTM D1003) indicates clarity - lower values provide better product visibility for retail. Gloss measurement 60-90% creates attractive shelf appeal. Transmission properties: clear film 90%+ light transmission, tinted films 40-70% for UV protection. Color consistency ŒîE<2 ensures brand color matching across production batches.

Barrier Properties: Water vapor transmission rate (WVTR) 5-50 g/m¬≤/day controls moisture ingress. Oxygen transmission rate (OTR) 1000-5000 cc/m¬≤/day affects oxidation-sensitive products. Thickness uniformity ¬±5% ensures consistent protection and machine performance. Heat seal strength 1-5 N/15mm provides secure closures for bags and overwraps.

Environmental Specifications

Temperature Resistance: Storage temperature range -40¬∞C to +60¬∞C for frozen goods transport and tropical climates. Shrink activation temperature 100-160¬∞C depending on film type. Low-temperature flexibility maintains pliability in cold storage facilities. High-temperature resistance to 80¬∞C for enclosed shipping containers in summer.

Environmental Compliance: Recyclable polyethylene marked with #2 or #4 resin codes for municipal recycling programs. Biodegradable oxo-degradable additives available breaking down film in 12-36 months for sustainable disposal. Heavy metal-free formulations comply with RoHS and REACH regulations. Food-contact approved materials certified to FDA 21 CFR 177.1520 and EU 10/2011.

Sustainability Options: Post-consumer recycled (PCR) content films contain 30-50% recycled polyethylene reducing virgin plastic consumption. Bio-based polyethylene from sugarcane ethanol offers carbon-neutral alternative. Downgauging initiatives reduce film thickness by 20-30% while maintaining performance using advanced polymers. Compostable PLA films for organic/food waste applications.

Application Equipment Compatibility

Manual Application: Hand-held dispenser reels with adjustable tension control for 400mm-500mm wide rolls. Ergonomic handles reduce operator fatigue during 8-hour shifts. Brake mechanisms control unwind speed preventing over-stretching. Compatible with standard 76mm (3") core diameter. Portable and battery-powered options for field/job-site use.

Semi-Automatic Wrapping: Turntable wrappers with programmable wrap patterns (top, bottom, full coverage) process 20-40 pallets/hour. Adjustable film tension and wrap overlap settings optimize material usage. Pre-stretch rollers amplify film to 250% reducing consumption. Photo-eye sensors detect pallet height for automatic top-off. Integrated film cutters and tail wipers complete professional finish.

Fully Automatic Systems: Rotary arm or ring wrappers integrate with conveyor lines processing 60-120 pallets/hour. RFID/barcode scanning coordinates with WMS for inventory control. Corner protection applicators attach cardboard or foam edge guards automatically. Film break detection with auto-restart minimizes downtime. Statistical process control monitors film usage per pallet for cost optimization.
